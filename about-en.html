About Us

About Basma

Basma is a strategic platform under C3 and the Women Future Cities initiative, designed to move women’s role in city futures from participation to leadership.

About the Summit

Basma brings women leaders, institutions, and development partners together to celebrate achievements and expand collaboration for smarter, more inclusive, and more sustainable cities.

Purpose

The Women Future Cities Summit highlights a full year of measurable achievements delivered by the community.

What Makes Basma Stand Out?

The event is focused on showcasing distinguished achievements by women, the community, and all partners who contributed to building and sustaining the initiative.

  • Integrated platform across policy, media, and innovation
  • Practical partnership and execution pathways
  • Awards that recognize measurable and sustainable impact

Management Message

Basma - Women Future Cities Summit & Awards is a leading strategic platform that captures the outcomes of a full year of tangible achievements in building future cities. The summit celebrates the pivotal role of women in leading change and innovation, while also spotlighting the high-value contributions of partners who supported the initiative and strengthened its sustainability.

Following the remarkable success of the first edition, we are pleased to extend this invitation for you to join us as partners in the second edition of the Basma Summit & Awards, and to continue this shared journey toward greater social impact and stronger women leadership in the transition toward smart and sustainable cities and communities.

We are confident that sustaining this partnership will expand the initiative’s impact and open new horizons for cross-sector collaboration, supporting development goals and helping build communities that are more innovative and sustainable.

Ms. Laila bint Abdullah Al-Hadhrami
Founder & Chairperson
